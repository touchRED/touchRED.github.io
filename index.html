Connor Trotter is a creative technologist and front end developer from Atlanta, GA. He's interested in using artificial intelligence and poetics to discover new systems of design and computation.

Right now he's working at Zero Studios in New York City. He studied Computer Science and Art at the University of Georgia, where you could usually find him around Ideas for Creative Exploration.

In the past he's created a vibrant web presence for a community space in Chinatown, investigated creative machine learning, built a generative collage tool, visualized connections between imagery and words, sorted the pixels of paintings, and written about bioinformatic complexity.

You can follow him on twitter, instagram, and github.
